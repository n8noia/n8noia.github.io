List of all the tournaments in NA/EMEA sponsored by Riot Games' Ignition series. This series was a way for eSports organizations to get involved with Riot and show that they have the ability to create and run tournaments. This series developed the VALORANT eSports scene as well as gave teams the chance to establish themselves as the top players in this young scene.
