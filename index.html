First time creating a character?

When you first dive in to the mystical and magical world of DND, there is a lot of paperwork to fill out. Creating a character is tedious work, and it's hard to understand if you're even doing it right. This website will take you through the process of creating a character step by step. I will also provide some insight to help you make those tough decisions This website has a ton of information, so don't fret if you don't get it at first.

Character Sheet

Your character sheet will be your guide for any information that you can't memorize. There are several ways to fill it out, but having a guide will make sure you have all the necessary items written down. Read more...

Race

DND has several customizable races with their own lore and stereotypes that help decide what kind of character you want to make. Each race comes with boons and banes that help shape your character and act as a springboard for writing your background. Read more...

Class

Like most RPG's, DND has several classes that you can pick from, as well as various subclasses to help shape your combat style. Choosing a class determines your character's specialties and fighting style. Read more...

Abilities

Every DND character has 6 ability scores and modifiers. These modifiers are physical and mental statistics that determine your character's ability to operate in the world. Read more...

Equipment

Throughout your campaign, you will encounter various weapons, armor, and magical items that aid you throughout your campaign. But your character has to start somewhere, and hopefully they aren't empty handed. Read more...
